Ponderless Guide

How to measure lap times with Ponderless

Ponderless is an AI-powered lap timer that measures rider passes using a single iPhone camera.

No transponder. No timing gate. Just place your phone beside the track and ride.

Designed for motocross, enduro, and solo practice sessions.

1. Place your phone beside the track
2. Start detection and ride past the camera
3. Open the pass list after your ride
4. Select photos to calculate lap times

Step-by-step guide

The images below show the typical flow inside the app.

Step 1

Place your phone and set the pass line

Put your iPhone beside the track where riders pass clearly in front of the camera. Adjust the detection line to the spot you want riders to cross.

Step 2

Start detection and ride past the camera

Tap the start button, then ride. Ponderless records pass moments automatically as riders cross the line.

Step 3

Open the pass list after your ride

The pass list shows captured pass photos in time order. Tap a photo to check it more closely.

Step 4

Select photos to calculate lap times

Choose two or more pass photos from the same rider, then calculate the lap times from those timestamps.

Result

Check your lap results

Ponderless shows your lap summary, including average, best, and each lap interval.

FAQ

Do I need a transponder or timing gate?

No. Ponderless is designed to work with just an iPhone camera.

Is it for motocross only?

It is designed for motocross, enduro, and practice riding where a single camera setup is useful.

Can I use it when riding alone?

Yes. That is one of the main use cases. Place the phone, ride, then review the captured passes after the session.
