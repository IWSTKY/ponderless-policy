<h1>Ponderless Privacy Policy</h1>


<p>Ponderless respects your privacy.</p>


<h2>Permissions</h2>

<p>Ponderless may access the following device features in order to provide app functionality:</p>

<ul>

<li>Camera – used to detect rider passes and measure lap times.</li>

<li>Photo Library – used to save captured images and videos.</li>

<li>Microphone – used when recording video with audio.</li>

</ul>


<h2>Data Processing</h2>

<ul>

<li>Rider detection and lap timing are processed locally on the device.</li>

<li>Ponderless does not sell personal data.</li>

<li>Ponderless does not use photos or videos for advertising.</li>

</ul>


<h2>Subscriptions</h2>

<p>Ponderless Pro is an optional auto-renewable subscription that unlocks additional features such as video recording and advanced analysis tools.</p>

<p>Payments and subscription management are handled through Apple's App Store.</p>


<h2>Data Storage</h2>

<p>Captured images, videos, sessions, and related app data may be stored locally on the user’s device.</p>


<h2>Third-Party Services</h2>

<p>In-app purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple.</p>


<h2>Your Choices</h2>

<ul>

<li>You can manage permissions in your device Settings.</li>

<li>You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.</li>

</ul>


<h2>Contact</h2>

<p>For questions regarding this policy, contact: support@ponderless.app</p>


<p>Effective date: 2026</p>
