Ponderless Privacy Policy

Ponderless respects your privacy.

Permissions

Ponderless may access the following device features in order to provide app functionality:

  • Camera – used to detect rider passes and measure lap times.
  • Photo Library – used to save captured images and videos.
  • Microphone – used when recording video with audio.

Data Processing

  • Rider detection and lap timing are processed locally on the device.
  • Ponderless does not sell personal data.
  • Ponderless does not use photos or videos for advertising.

Subscriptions

Ponderless Pro is an optional auto-renewable subscription that unlocks additional features such as video recording and advanced analysis tools.

Payments and subscription management are handled through Apple's App Store.

Data Storage

Captured images, videos, sessions, and related app data may be stored locally on the user’s device.

Third-Party Services

In-app purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple.

Your Choices

  • You can manage permissions in your device Settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy, contact: support@ponderless.app

Effective date: 2026
